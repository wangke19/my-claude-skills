# Checkpoint Template

Use at the end of every chapter to verify mastery before advancing.

## Questions (verbal/written)

1. Explain the core concept in your own words — no looking at notes
2. What are the prerequisites for this concept?
3. What problem does it solve, and what would go wrong without it?

## Hands-on Task

One independent mini-task the learner completes without guidance. Must be:
- Completable in under 10 minutes
- Verifiable (clear right/wrong outcome)
- Novel (not a repeat of the example)

## Pass Criteria

- [ ] Can explain the concept clearly
- [ ] Can execute the task without prompting
- [ ] Can identify and fix a deliberate mistake in a given snippet

If the learner fails any criterion, backtrack one concept and re-teach with a different analogy or approach.
